# Phonology

## Sound Inventory
Consonants:
- `x` - /x/
- `t` - /t/
- `l` - /l/
- `z` - /ɕ/
- `k` - /k/
- `s` - /s/
- `p` - /p/
- `v` - /v/
- `g` - /ŋ/
- `n` - /n/
- `r` - /r/ or /ɾ/
- `b` - /b/
- `j` - /j/
- `m` - /m/
- Between adjacent vowels - /ʔ/

Vowels:
- `e` - /ɛ/
- `a` - /ə/
- `o` - /o/
- `i` - /i/
- `u` - /ʊ/
